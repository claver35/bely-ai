Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We do not sell your data. We do not share your customers' data with anyone. You can delete your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

BELY AI ("we", "us", "our") is a Shopify risk analysis service available at bely-ai.vercel.app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your customers.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Supabase.

Shopify Store Data: When you connect your Shopify store, we access:

  • Order data (order ID, amount, date, status)
  • Customer information (name, email, shipping address, IP address)
  • Payment method details (card type, last 4 digits — never full card numbers)
  • Order risk indicators provided by Shopify

Usage Data: We collect basic analytics about how you use our dashboard (page views, feature usage) to improve the Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the risk scoring service
  • Generate AI-powered risk assessments for your orders
  • Send you alerts about high-risk orders (if enabled)
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Respond to support requests

We never use your customers' data to train general AI models or for any purpose outside of providing your risk analysis.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with the following trusted service providers necessary to operate BELY AI:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions
  • Paddle — payment processing (they handle billing data under their own privacy policy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously. Your Shopify access tokens are stored encrypted. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We use Supabase's row-level security to ensure you can only access your own data. We regularly review our security practices.

6. Data Retention

We retain your order data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription, your data is retained for 30 days to allow for reactivation, then permanently deleted. You can request immediate deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@belyai.com.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect your ability to log in.

9. Children's Privacy

BELY AI is a B2B service intended for merchants. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of material changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Privacy questions or data requests? Contact us at support@belyai.com
