Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026
BELY AI ("we", "our", "us") operates a Shopify fraud detection application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our service. By connecting your Shopify store to BELY AI, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Shopify Store Data

When you connect your Shopify store via OAuth, we access the following data through Shopify's API using the read_orders scope:

  • Order numbers, amounts, dates, and financial status
  • Customer account creation date and order count
  • Billing and shipping country/city (not full address)
  • Payment gateway name (e.g., "Shopify Payments", "PayPal")
  • Product names, quantities, and prices (Elite plan only)
  • Customer name and email address (Elite plan only)
  • Refund and fulfillment status
⚠️ We never access or store: credit card numbers, CVV codes, full payment details, Shopify admin passwords, or any financial credentials.

1.2 Account Information

When you create a BELY AI account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. We use Supabase Auth for authentication, which handles password storage securely.

1.3 OAuth Access Token

Your Shopify OAuth access token is stored encrypted on our servers. It is never sent to the browser or exposed in client-side code. It is used solely to fetch order data from Shopify's API on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Fraud risk scoring: We analyze order signals to calculate a risk score (LOW / MED / HIGH) for each order.
  • Account health monitoring: We calculate your chargeback ratio and warn you before reaching Shopify's thresholds.
  • Email alerts: We send fraud alert emails to your registered address when high or medium risk orders are detected (Pro and Elite plans).
  • Chargeback reporting: We record refund events to provide historical chargeback analysis (Elite plan).
  • Service improvement: We use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our fraud detection algorithms. No individual store data is used for this purpose.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

✅ We do not sell, rent, or share your store data with any third party for commercial purposes.

We use the following sub-processors to operate our service:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (EU data centers, GDPR compliant)
  • Vercel — API hosting and edge network (SOC2 certified)
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (alert emails only)
  • Paddle — Payment processing and subscription management
  • Shopify — Source of order data via official API

Each sub-processor is bound by their own privacy and security commitments. We share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in Supabase PostgreSQL databases located in EU data centers.
  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 / 256-bit SSL.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — each user can only access their own store's data.
  • API endpoints are protected by rate limiting (Cloudflare WAF) and HMAC signature verification for webhooks.
  • Shopify access tokens are stored server-side only and never exposed to client-side JavaScript.

5. Data Retention

  • Order risk data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Chargeback records: Retained while your account is active.
  • Shopify access token: Retained until you disconnect your store.
  • Account data (email): Retained until account deletion.
  • All data upon deletion: Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of all your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@belyshield.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Deletion Requests

You may request full deletion of your data at any time by:

  • Emailing info@belyshield.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Including your registered email address and Shopify store domain

All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

8. Security Breach Notification

In the event of a data security breach that affects your personal data, we will:

  • Notify affected users by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.
  • Provide details of what data was affected and what steps we are taking.
  • Report to relevant data protection authorities as required by applicable law.

9. Shopify App Specific Disclosures

This application is built on the Shopify Partner platform. We comply with Shopify's API Terms of Service and Shopify's Privacy Policy.

  • We only request the minimum OAuth scopes necessary: read_orders
  • We do not access Protected Customer Data beyond what is explicitly approved by Shopify.
  • We do not use Shopify merchant or customer data for purposes other than providing the BELY AI fraud detection service.
  • Merchants can revoke our access at any time through their Shopify admin panel.

10. Cookies

BELY AI does not use tracking or advertising cookies. We use Supabase Auth for authentication, which stores an essential session cookie to keep you logged in. We also use browser localStorage to store your connected store domain for session continuity. No advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking cookies are used.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email. Continued use of BELY AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
