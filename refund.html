Refund Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

Simple version: If you're not satisfied within 7 days of your first payment, contact us and we'll refund you — no questions asked.

1. Free Trial

BELY AI offers a 3-day free trial when you first connect your Shopify store. You will not be charged during this period. No credit card is required to start your trial. If you do not subscribe before the trial ends, your account will simply be downgraded — you will never be automatically charged.

2. Subscription Refunds

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first payment. If you are not satisfied with BELY AI for any reason within 7 days of your first subscription charge, contact us at support@belyai.com and we will issue a full refund.

For subsequent billing periods, refunds are generally not provided. However, we review refund requests on a case-by-case basis. If you experience a technical issue that prevented you from using the Service, we may issue a partial or full refund at our discretion.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@belyai.com
  • Include the email address associated with your account
  • Briefly describe the reason for your refund request

We will process your request within 3 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank.

4. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future charges immediately. You will continue to have access to BELY AI until the end of your current billing period. We do not charge cancellation fees.

5. Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Requests made more than 7 days after the charge date (for first payments) or after the billing period has ended
  • Dissatisfaction with risk scoring decisions, as these are informational tools and accuracy cannot be guaranteed

6. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by Paddle, our authorized reseller. Paddle acts as the Merchant of Record for all BELY AI transactions. If you have questions about a specific charge, you can also contact Paddle's support directly. Your subscription will appear as "Paddle.com" on your bank statement.

Refund requests or billing questions? Email us at support@belyai.com — we respond within 24 hours.
