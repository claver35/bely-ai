§ 06 · FAQ

Straight answers.

How does BELY AI score an order?

We evaluate 40+ signals in under 200ms: IP geolocation vs. shipping address, email age, device fingerprint, card velocity, order pattern deviation, and behavioral cues from the checkout session. The score comes back before your fulfillment flow even starts.

What if Shopify's already flagged my store?

We can still help — tighter defense thresholds immediately bring your ratio down. Most accounts recover in 30–45 days with aggressive scoring + AI dispute responses. We'll give you an honest read during onboarding.

Will you slow down my checkout?

No. Risk scoring happens post-checkout, pre-fulfillment. Shoppers see no extra step. You see a score in your dashboard within seconds of an order landing.

What happens when a chargeback actually lands?

Our AI drafts a response letter with the right evidence (shipping proof, device match, order history, communication logs) attached — you review and submit in two clicks. Pro plan submits automatically.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II and only process data needed for risk scoring. Nothing is sold, nothing leaves our infrastructure, and you can request deletion at any time.
