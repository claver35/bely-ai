§ 05 · FAQ

Straight answers.

How does BELY AI score a Shopify order?

We pull order data from Shopify's API: IP address, account age, address match, payment method, velocity, and disposable email detection. Score assigned in under 200ms.

Will you slow down my checkout?

Zero impact. Risk scoring happens post-order, pre-fulfillment.

Is my Shopify store data safe?

Yes. API keys stored encrypted server-side. We never sell or share your data.

What if Shopify has already flagged my account?

Most accounts recover in 30–45 days with aggressive scoring + AI dispute responses.
