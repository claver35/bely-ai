§ 05 · FAQ

Straight answers.

How does BELY AI score an order?

We pull order data from Shopify's API the moment a new order is placed: IP address, customer account age, email, shipping vs billing address match, payment method, order velocity, and more. Our algorithm assigns a 0–100 risk score in under 200ms. HIGH risk orders trigger an immediate alert so you can hold shipment before goods leave your warehouse.

Can BELY AI access my customer's device fingerprint?

Standard Shopify API provides IP, account data, and order signals — which alone give us strong scoring power. Deep device fingerprinting (browser fonts, hardware ID) requires a Script Tag installation in your storefront, which is our next feature release. For now, our current algorithm catches the vast majority of fraud with the data Shopify provides.

What if Shopify has already flagged my account?

We can still help. Tighter defense thresholds immediately reduce your chargeback ratio. Most accounts recover in 30–45 days with aggressive pre-shipment scoring + AI dispute responses. We'll give you an honest assessment when you connect your store.

Will you slow down my checkout?

Zero impact on checkout. Risk scoring happens post-order, pre-fulfillment — entirely on our servers. Shoppers see no extra step. You see a risk score in your dashboard within seconds of an order landing.

Is my store data safe?

Yes. API keys are stored encrypted in server environment variables — never in client-side code. All analysis runs server-side on Vercel's infrastructure. We process only the order data needed for risk scoring and never sell or share your data. You can request deletion at any time.

What's your promise, exactly?

Not "we'll protect you 100%." Our promise is: we give you the insight Shopify doesn't give you — deep order risk analysis, pre-shipment alerts, and AI-drafted dispute responses. Think of us as an always-on alarm system, not an insurance policy. The decisions remain yours; we just make sure you're making them with full information.
