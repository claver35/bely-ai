§ 05 · FAQ

Straight answers.

How does BELY AI score an order?

We pull order data from Shopify's API: IP address, account age, address match, payment method, order velocity. Score assigned in under 200ms. HIGH risk triggers an immediate alert.

Will you slow down my checkout?

Zero impact. Risk scoring happens post-order, pre-fulfillment — entirely on our servers.

Is my store data safe?

Yes. API keys stored encrypted server-side. We never sell or share your data. You can request deletion at any time.

What if Shopify has already flagged my account?

We can still help. Most accounts recover in 30–45 days with aggressive pre-shipment scoring + AI dispute responses.
